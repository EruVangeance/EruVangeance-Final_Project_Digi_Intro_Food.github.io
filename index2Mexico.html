Mexican Food

Mexican cuisine is known for its vibrant flavors and colorful presentation. It is a blend of indigenous Mesoamerican and European (mainly Spanish) culinary traditions. Some popular Mexican dishes include tacos, burritos, enchiladas, and quesadillas.
